Stir-fried Fish Cakes

A quick and common side dish consisting of sliced fish cakes stir-fried with vegetables in a savory sauce.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Stir-fried Fish Cakes.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 200 g Korean fish cakes
  • Onion, carrot, green onion (a small amount each)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1‚Äì2 tbsp water
  • Sesame oil & sesame seeds

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace fish cakes with tofu noodles, mushrooms, or vegetables such as bell peppers.
  • Halal substitution: Use halal-certified fish cakes. If unavailable, check that soy sauce used is halal-certified (conditional halal).
  • Allergy substitution: For fish, wheat, or soy allergies, replace fish cakes with tofu noodles, rice noodles, or vegetables.

Instructions

  1. Slice fish cakes and vegetables into thin strips.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, stir-fry onion and carrot first, then add fish cakes.
  3. Add soy sauce, sugar, and water, and stir-fry over high heat.
  4. Finish with sesame oil and sesame seeds.
