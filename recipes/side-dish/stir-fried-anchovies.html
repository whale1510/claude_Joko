A popular savory and sweet side dish made from small dried anchovies coated in a sticky soy sauce glaze.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Stir-fried Anchovies.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 80 g small dried anchovies
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup or sugar
  • Sesame oil & sesame seeds

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace anchovies with nuts such as almonds or walnuts, or mushrooms like shiitake.
  • Halal substitution: Anchovies are generally halal, as most seafood is permitted.
  • Allergy substitution: For fish allergies, remove anchovies or substitute with mushrooms or nuts.

Instructions

  1. Toast anchovies in a dry pan over low heat for 2‚Äì3 minutes to remove the fishy smell.
  2. Add a little oil and stir-fry until lightly crispy.
  3. Add soy sauce and corn syrup; stir quickly to coat evenly.
  4. Remove from heat and finish with sesame oil and sesame seeds.
