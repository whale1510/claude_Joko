Fresh Serve

Highlight the fresh color to create a refreshing side dish.

  • Serve on a white plate to emphasize the green contrast.
  • Add a touch of lemon zest or yuzu syrup for aroma.
  • Replace sesame seeds with sliced toasted almonds for a different texture.

Ingredients

  • 200g spinach
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • Salt to taste

Substitution Tips

  • Soy sauce can be replaced with tamari or low-sodium soy sauce.
  • Use perilla oil or mild grapeseed oil instead of sesame oil.
  • Add ginger juice instead of minced garlic for a fresh flavor.

Instructions

  1. Add a pinch of salt to boiling water and blanch spinach for 30 seconds.
  2. Rinse in cold water, squeeze out excess moisture, and cut into bite-sized lengths.
  3. In a bowl, mix spinach with soy sauce and minced garlic lightly.
  4. Add sesame oil and toasted sesame seeds for aroma, then transfer to a serving dish.
