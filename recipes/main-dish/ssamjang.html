Ssamjang

Quickly make this sweet and savory dipping sauce that pairs perfectly with fresh lettuce wraps.

Pairing Ideas

Use ssamjang in various ways to complete a bountiful spread.

  • Serve with lettuce or perilla leaves for instant wraps.
  • Use as a sauce for grilled vegetables or tofu steaks.
  • Mix with vegan yogurt to transform it into a dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons doenjang (Korean soybean paste)
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds

Substitution Tips

  • Gochujang: Choose vegan-certified or halal-certified red chili paste.
  • Soy sauce: Substitute with low-sodium soy sauce or tamari for a gluten-free option.
  • Sesame oil: Can be replaced with perilla oil or mild avocado oil.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix doenjang and gochujang evenly.
  2. Add soy sauce, minced garlic, and minced green onion to create a smooth texture.
  3. Add sesame oil and toasted sesame seeds at the end, mixing to enhance the aroma.
  4. Adjust sweetness to taste by adding a small amount of honey or maesil-cheong (plum syrup).
