Spicy Stir-fried Pork

A popular Korean dish featuring sliced pork marinated and stir-fried in a spicy gochujang-based sauce.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Spicy Stir-fried Pork.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 200g sliced pork (shoulder or belly)
  • 2 tbsp gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru (red chili flakes)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 carrot (sliced, optional)
  • 1 green onion (sliced)

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace pork with tofu, soy meat, or mushrooms.
  • Halal substitution: Replace pork with halal-certified beef or chicken.
  • Allergy substitution: For gochujang or soy sauce, use halal-certified products that do not contain wheat or soy.

Instructions

  1. Mix pork with gochujang, soy sauce, sugar, garlic, and chili flakes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add onions and pork mixture; stir-fry on medium-high heat.
  4. Cook until pork is fully done.
  5. Add green onion and cook 1 more minute.
