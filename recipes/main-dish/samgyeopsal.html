Thick slices of pork belly, grilled until crispy, usually served with ssamjang and wrapped in fresh greens.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Samgyeopsal.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 200‚Äì300g pork belly
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ssamjang (for serving)
  • Lettuce or perilla leaves (for wrapping)

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace pork with thick tofu or mushroom steaks.
  • Halal substitution: Replace pork with halal-certified lamb or beef.
  • Allergy substitution: For ssamjang, use halal-certified products that do not contain wheat or soy.

Instructions

  1. Heat a grill pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Place pork belly slices and cook until browned and crispy.
  3. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve with lettuce and ssamjang.
