Dak-galbi

‚è± Prep 20 min + Cook 20 min ü•Ñ Serves 3

A spicy stir-fry made with chicken, cabbage, sweet potatoes, and other vegetables marinated in gochujang.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Dak-galbi.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken thigh (bite-sized)
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru
  • 1/2 cup cabbage
  • 1/4 sweet potato (sliced)
  • 1/2 onion

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan Substitution
    • Chicken ‚Üí Tofu
    • Chicken ‚Üí Soy meat
    • Chicken ‚Üí Potatoes
  • Halal Substitution
    • Chicken ‚Üí Halal-certified chicken
  • Allergy Substitution
    • Gochujang / Soy Sauce ‚Üí Use halal-certified products
    • Choose options without wheat or soy

Instructions

  1. Mix chicken with all sauces and garlic.
  2. Heat the pan and add chicken.
  3. Add cabbage, onion, and sweet potato.
  4. Stir-fry until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Add a little water if needed.
