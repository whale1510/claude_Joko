Bulgogi

Thinly sliced, marinated beef grilled or pan-fried, famous for its sweet and savory soy sauce flavor.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Bulgogi.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 200g thinly sliced beef
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp cooking wine (optional)
  • 1/4 onion (sliced)
  • 1/4 carrot (thinly sliced)
  • 1 green onion
  • Sesame seeds

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace beef with tofu, soy meat, or shiitake mushrooms.
  • Halal substitution: Use halal-certified beef instead of regular beef. Replace cooking wine with vinegar or grape juice.
  • Allergy substitution: For soy sauce, use rice-based soy sauce without wheat. For sesame oil, use olive oil if allergic to sesame.

Instructions

  1. Mix beef with soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and garlic.
  2. Add onion, carrot, and green onion to marinade.
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  4. Cook marinated beef until browned.
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds before serving.
