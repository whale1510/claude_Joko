Make this iconic Korean rice bowl at home with a colorful array of vegetable side dishes and savory gochujang sauce.

Bowl Composition

Create an appetizing bowl by showcasing vibrant colors.

  • Arrange red, green, and yellow toppings evenly to create color contrast.
  • Use a stone bowl or wide dish for easy mixing and to keep the dish warm longer.
  • Finish with sesame oil and seaweed flakes for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 bowls warm cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup each: seasoned spinach, seasoned bean sprouts, sauteed zucchini, sauteed carrot
  • 3 tbsp gochujang sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 fried eggs

Substitution Tips

  • Make a simple gochujang sauce by mixing vegan gochujang, maple syrup, and sesame oil.
  • Replace eggs with grilled tofu or shiitake mushrooms for a vegan version.
  • Use multigrain rice or cauliflower rice to adjust the nutritional profile.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetable side dishes and sauteed toppings in advance, seasoning each separately.
  2. Place warm rice in a bowl and arrange toppings evenly on top.
  3. Add gochujang sauce and drizzle with sesame oil to taste, then mix well before eating.
