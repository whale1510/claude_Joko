Kimchi Fried Rice

A quick and satisfying meal made with spicy kimchi, rice, and simple ingredients.

Flavor Boost

Finishing touches to enhance the flavor of your fried rice.

  • Press kimchi against the edges of the pan to create a charred, smoky flavor.
  • Top generously with seaweed flakes or finely chopped green onions for color.
  • Add a drizzle of vegan butter or sesame oil for extra richness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup well-fermented kimchi
  • 2 bowls cold cooked rice
  • 1/2 onion, 1/2 green onion
  • 1 tbsp gochujang, 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 fried eggs (optional)

Substitution Tips

  • Use gochujang sauce instead of plain gochujang, or mix in a bit of tomato paste if you want to reduce the spice level.
  • Skip ham or tuna and add tofu or chickpeas for a vegan version with plenty of protein.
  • Replace eggs with grilled mushrooms or avocado for a unique twist.

Instructions

  1. Chop the kimchi finely and dice the onion and green onion.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a pan and stir-fry kimchi and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add rice and mix well, then season with gochujang and soy sauce.
  4. Add green onion at the end, turn off the heat, and serve in a bowl topped with fried eggs or alternative toppings.
