A simple, popular rice dish topped with a creamy, seasoned mixture of canned tuna and mayonnaise.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Tuna Mayo Rice Bowl.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl steamed rice
  • 1 can tuna (drained)
  • 1‚Äì2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp minced onion (optional)
  • Seaweed flakes & green onion

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace tuna with soy meat flakes, tofu scramble, or tempeh. Use vegan mayonnaise instead of regular mayonnaise.
  • Halal substitution: Tuna is generally halal, as most seafood is permitted. Check that soy sauce and mayonnaise are halal-certified (conditional halal).
  • Allergy substitution: For fish allergies, remove tuna or substitute with vegetables or meat. For soy sauce, use wheat-free rice-based soy sauce.

Instructions

  1. Mix tuna with mayonnaise, soy sauce, and sugar.
  2. Place warm rice in a bowl.
  3. Top with the tuna mixture.
  4. Garnish with seaweed flakes and green onion.
