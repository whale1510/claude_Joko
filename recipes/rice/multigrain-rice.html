Healthy rice blend combining white rice with various grains like brown rice, barley, and beans for added nutrients and texture.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Multigrain Rice.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed grains (brown rice, barley, black rice, beans)
  • 2 1/4 cups water

Substitution Tips

  • Allergy substitution: For specific grain allergies (beans or barley), remove the respective grain.

Instructions

  1. Rinse white rice and mixed grains separately.
  2. Combine the grains and add water.
  3. Cook in a rice cooker or simmer on low heat until tender.
  4. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
