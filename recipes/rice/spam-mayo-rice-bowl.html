A quick, savory rice bowl topped with crispy fried Spam, egg, and a drizzle of mayonnaise and soy sauce.

Cooking Tips

Tips for making delicious Spam Mayo Rice Bowl.

  • Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
  • Serve immediately for optimal taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl steamed rice
  • 4‚Äì5 slices Spam (diced)
  • 1 egg (sunny side up or scrambled)
  • 1‚Äì2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Soy sauce drizzle
  • Seaweed flakes & green onion

Substitution Tips

  • Vegan substitution: Replace Spam with vegan Spam or soy meat. Replace egg with tofu scramble or a vegan egg substitute. Use vegan mayonnaise instead of regular mayonnaise.
  • Halal substitution: Replace Spam with halal-certified sausage or ham (no pork content). Check that soy sauce and mayonnaise are halal-certified (conditional halal).
  • Allergy substitution: For meat or wheat allergies, remove Spam or use a vegan substitute. For egg allergy, replace egg with tofu scramble.

Instructions

  1. Pan-fry diced Spam until golden and crispy.
  2. Place warm rice in a bowl.
  3. Add the fried Spam and the cooked egg.
  4. Drizzle mayonnaise and a little soy sauce.
  5. Finish with seaweed flakes and green onion.
